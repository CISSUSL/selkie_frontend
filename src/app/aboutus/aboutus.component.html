About Us.

Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, from its inception in 1996, has for more than one and a half decades dedicated its mission towards nurturing scholars who could contribute to society, by broadening knowledge paradigms and imparting it to new generations of students.

The Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka which is well known for its natural beauty has completed 16 years of operation. Within this short period of time, the university has become a center of academic excellence in higher education and has reward significant achievements in teaching, research & other key areas within the higher educational system. As one of the fastest growing universities, it inculcates a sense of social responsibility, service & commitment to the democratic values of the society, both locally & internationally among its students.

At present, Sabaragamuwa University has 6 main faculties including Faculty of Applied Sciences, Faculty of Geomatics, Faculty of Agricultural Sciences, Faculty of Management Studies, Faculty of Social Sciences & Languages and Faculty of Graduate Studies.

SUSL also has several other institutes & centers which are in great demand for both the undergraduates & postgraduates.

SUSL tends to encourage a close relationship between university and its alumni. With that they can increase alumni interest towards university events  affairs and also build a relationship among them self. University can involve them for the future developments and betterment of the university and also alumni can also get benefits in return.
